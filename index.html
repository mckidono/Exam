There are 3 entries into the demon souls universe, each one of the games, you play as the chosen undead that is tasked with either linking the fire and continuing the age of gods or to extinguish the fire and lead the world into the age of humanity. Throughout all the games you encounter many different races, wepons, and spells. Some examples of races that you will meet in the game are Hollow, Humans, Dragons, Demons, Half-Breeds, Gods, Lords, Giants, Undead, and Abyss
